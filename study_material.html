Study Tips & Guidelines

1. Create a consistent study schedule and stick to it
2. Use active learning techniques (summarizing, teaching others)
3. Take regular breaks using the Pomodoro technique (25min work, 5min break)
4. Organize study materials by subject and topic
5. Practice with past papers and mock exams
6. Form study groups for difficult subjects
7. Use flashcards for memorization
8. Stay hydrated and maintain a healthy diet
9. Get adequate sleep before exams
10. Ask teachers for clarification when needed
