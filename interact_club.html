About Us

The interact Club is a student-led organization dedicated to exploring technology, innovation, and creative problem-solving.

Our Mission

Join the Club

Become a member of Interact Club and start your tech journey today!

Why Join?

Membership in Interact Club offers numerous benefits:

  • Learn valuable tech skills (coding, robotics, AI)
  • Work on hands-on projects with mentorship
  • Participate in competitions and hackathons
  • Connect with industry professionals
  • Build your portfolio for college applications
  • Make friends with similar interests

Membership Requirements

Open to all St Peter's Nsambya students. No prior experience needed! Just bring your curiosity and willingness to learn.

Annual Membership Fee

$20 for the entire school year (covers materials, snacks, and event costs). Fee waivers available - see club advisor.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the interact Club.

Who can join the interact Club?

The club is open to all St Peter's Nsambya students regardless of grade level or prior experience with technology. We welcome beginners and experienced tech enthusiasts alike!

When and where does the club meet?

We meet every Friday from 3:30 PM to 5:00 PM in the Science Building, Room 205. Additional workshop sessions may be scheduled throughout the week.

Is there a membership fee?

Yes, there is an annual membership fee of $20 to cover materials, snacks, and event costs. However, fee waivers are available for students who need financial assistance. Please contact the club advisor for more information.

What if I have no coding or tech experience?

No problem! We offer beginner workshops and have mentorship programs where experienced members help newcomers. Everyone starts somewhere, and our club is the perfect place to begin your tech journey.

What kind of projects does the club work on?

Our projects vary based on member interests but typically include: web development, mobile apps, robotics, AI projects, game development, 3D printing, and IoT (Internet of Things) devices. Members can also propose their own passion projects!

How can I get more involved in club leadership?

We elect new officers each spring for the following school year. Additionally, we have various committee positions available throughout the year. Talk to current club officers or the advisor to express your interest.
