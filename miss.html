Competition Guidelines

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a current student of St Peters SSS Nsambya
  • Minimum academic performance of 2.5 GPA
  • Good disciplinary record
  • Active participation in school activities
  • Leadership qualities demonstrated

Competition Timeline

  • Nomination Period: Sept 1 - Sept 30
  • Screening & Interviews: Oct 1 - Oct 15
  • Campaign Period: Oct 16 - Nov 15
  • Voting Period: Nov 16 - Nov 30
  • Results Announcement: Dec 10

Voting Rules

  • One vote per student per category
  • Voting requires student ID verification
  • Voting closes at 11:59 PM on deadline
  • Results are final and binding
  • No vote buying or coercion allowed
