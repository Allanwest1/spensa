Athletics Class of 2010

David Omondi

Olympic Gold Medalist

"The track taught me discipline; my teachers taught me perseverance."

Won gold in 400m at Tokyo 2020 Olympics. Holds 3 African records and represented Uganda in 15 international competitions.

Arts Class of 2005

Grace Wangari

Renowned Visual Artist

"Art allows us to see the world through different lenses."

Exhibited in 12 countries, with works displayed in the National Gallery. Founded the "Art for Education" initiative supporting young artists.

Hall of Fame since 2021
Community Class of 1978

Dr. Michael Okoth

Public Health Pioneer

"Service to others is the rent we pay for our room on earth."

Established 5 rural health clinics serving over 50,000 people. Led vaccination campaigns reducing disease rates by 60% in northern Uganda.

Hall of Fame since 2018
Leadership Class of 1995

Rebecca Atim

Supreme Court Justice

"True leadership is about making justice accessible to all."

First female Supreme Court Justice in Uganda's history. Authored landmark rulings on gender equality and children's rights.
